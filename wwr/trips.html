Featured Trip Packages

Salmon River Splash

Duration: 1 Day | Difficulty: Easy (Class I-II)

Perfect for families and beginners. Enjoy a scenic float down calm waters with gentle splashes, beautiful canyon views, and a riverside lunch stop.

Canyon Fury Run

Duration: 2 Days | Difficulty: Moderate (Class III-IV)

For the thrill-seekers looking for real adrenaline. Navigate intense drop-pools, technical boulder fields, and camp under the stars overnight.

The Devil's Snout Challenge

Duration: 3 Days | Difficulty: Hard (Class IV-V)

An extreme expedition for experienced rafters. Battle massive waves, fast-moving technical currents, and continuous whitewater rapids.
